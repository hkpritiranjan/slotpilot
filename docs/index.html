How it works

Three steps. One click from your side.

Everything after step two is automatic. Your receptionist never calls a patient to fill a gap again.

1
Patients join your waitlist
You get a shareable link — /join/your-clinic. Share it on WhatsApp, in your email signature, or on a card at the front desk. Patients fill in their name, mobile, and what appointment they need. No app download. Done in 90 seconds.
One-time setup
2
A slot opens
When a patient cancels or doesn’t show up, open SlotPilot and click Cancel or No-show on that slot. That’s the one action your staff takes.
15 seconds of staff time
3
SlotPilot handles the rest
The system finds the right patient (matching appointment type + priority), texts them instantly, waits for their YES or NO, books the slot, sends a confirmation, and schedules their 24h + 2h reminders. All automatic.
Zero staff involvement
Features

Everything a small clinic actually needs

No hospital software. No ₹50,000/year enterprise plan. Just the tools to fill your empty slots and reduce no-shows.

⚡
Automatic SMS cascade
The moment you cancel a slot, the system finds the next matching patient and texts them — no human in the loop.
⏱
15-minute response window
Patient gets 15 minutes to reply YES. No response or a NO? The next person on the list is tried automatically.
🔔
24h + 2h reminders
Every booked patient gets an automatic reminder the day before and 2 hours before — reducing no-show rates by 30–40%.
⇧
Priority queue
Mark urgent or post-surgery patients as high priority. They jump the queue and get offered slots before anyone else.
🔗
Public join link
Your unique waitlist link works on any device, any browser. Patients don’t need to create an account or download anything.
🏥
Multi-practitioner
Manage slots across multiple doctors or physios in one clinic. Each slot can be assigned to a specific practitioner.
💬
WhatsApp integration
Indians respond to WhatsApp, not SMS. Full WhatsApp Business API integration via MSG91 or Gupshup.
